Wanna go but aren't sure what to wear? Check out our inspiration gallery for ideas. For authenticy, check out the recesses of dear old dad's closet or your Aunt Sally's boudoir.

Big Brothers Big Sisters

Still on the fence about attending? Know that proceeds from this event go directly to the Big Brothers Big Sisters Program of Greater Tarrant County.

BBBS focuses on pairing at-risk youth with one-on-one longterm mentors (at least a year).

There are over 700 kids on the waiting list in DFW.

It costs approx. $1,300 to match a child with a mentor and service that match for one year.

These kids are 90% less likely to be incarcerated after being paired with a long-term mentor.
