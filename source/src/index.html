Big Brothers Big Sisters

Still on the fence about attending? Know that proceeds from this event go toward supporting the Big Brothers Big Sisters Program of Greater Tarrant County.

BBBS focuses on pairing at-risk youth with one-on-one long term mentors.

The Facts:

  • There are over 700 kids on the waiting list in DFW.
  • These kids are 90% less likely to be incarcerated after being paired with a long-term mentor.
  • It costs approximately $1,300 to match a child with a mentor and service that match for one year.
  • Youth incarceration costs the state over $100,000 annually, per child.

Want to make a real difference to someone with real needs?
